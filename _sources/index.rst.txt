LabGraph is a streaming framework built by the Facebook Reality Labs Research team at Facebook.
LabGraph is mentioned in this story on `the potential of brain-computer interfaces for restoring speech communication <https://tech.fb.com/ar-vr/2021/07/bci-milestone-new-research-from-ucsf-with-support-from-facebook-shows-the-potential-of-brain-computer-interfaces-for-restoring-speech-communication/>`_. More information can be found in Introduction
